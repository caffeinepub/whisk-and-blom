# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create an e-commerce website for Whisk and Bloom, a natural food colour brand, with product browsing, shopping cart, and checkout functionality.

**Planned changes:**
- Build homepage with hero section, product highlights, and brand story
- Create product catalog page displaying natural food colour products with images, names, descriptions, and prices
- Implement shopping cart system allowing users to add products, view cart contents, update quantities, and see total price
- Build checkout flow for reviewing orders, entering shipping information, and completing purchases
- Create individual product detail pages with full information and add to cart functionality
- Design cohesive visual theme with warm earth tones, botanical elements, and clean modern typography

**User-visible outcome:** Users can browse natural food colour products, view detailed product information, add items to cart, and complete purchases through a visually appealing e-commerce website with a natural, organic aesthetic.
